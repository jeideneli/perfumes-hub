The Art of Fragrance

In the heart of Provence, where lavender fields meet ancient olive groves, our journey began with a simple dream: to capture the essence of beauty in a bottle.

Our Heritage

Founded by master perfumer Isabelle Dubois in 1987, Lumière Parfums has been crafting exceptional fragrances for over three decades. Each creation is a testament to our commitment to artistry, quality, and the timeless beauty of natural ingredients.

The Craft

We source the world's finest raw materials, from Bulgarian rose petals harvested at dawn to rare oud from ancient trees. Our master perfumers blend tradition with innovation, creating fragrances that are both contemporary and timeless.

Our Philosophy

We believe that fragrance is more than a scent—it's a memory, an emotion, a story waiting to be told. Every Lumière perfume is designed to evoke feelings, create connections, and become part of your personal narrative.
